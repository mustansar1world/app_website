Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Civil Engineering Calculator ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by our team. The Application is a suite of calculators designed for civil engineers, surveyors, and construction professionals to perform beam analysis, concrete estimations, coordinate surveying, chain corrections, and other engineering calculations on site.

1. What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information: The Application does not require any user registration. You do not need to provide any personal information to use the core features of the Application.

Automatically Collected Information: The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device's unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, and information about the way you use the Application. This information is collected solely to analyze trends, administer the app, and improve overall performance.

2. Does the Application collect precise real-time location information?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device. If any surveying tools require local inputs (like slope, angles, or coordinates), these are entered manually by the user or loaded from CSV files provided by the user. They are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to any external servers.

3. Third Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you. Link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the Application:

4. PDF Report Generation & Device Storage

The Application requires permission to write to your device's storage/downloads folder in order to generate and save PDF reports (e.g., Chain Correction reports) and save exported Contour Plot bitmap images. These files remain on your device unless you choose to share them using the system share manager.

5. Data Retention Policy

Since we do not collect or store personal data, we do not retain any of your private information. All calculations, coordinate inputs, and generated plots reside locally in the memory of your device and are cleared or saved as per your device's operating system guidelines.

6. Security

We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information we process and maintain. For example, we limit access to this information to authorized employees and contractors who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve our Application.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
